Research & Selected Projects

ML-Enhanced Factor-Model Portfolio Optimization

Predict-then-optimize: LSTM + Black–Litterman, CVaR, robust SOCP
Cornell ORIE 5370 · 2026

Built a rolling daily backtest (2022–2023) comparing ten portfolio strategies fed the same LSTM return forecasts and factor-model covariance. Black–Litterman shrinkage of noisy ML views toward market equilibrium delivered the best risk-adjusted performance (gross Sharpe 0.67) with lower drawdown and better diversification; validated with stationary block-bootstrap significance tests and transaction-cost analysis.
